findMe is a mobile community that shows you all registered users in your neighborhood, city, country or anywhere in the entire world!
You can see the position of your friends on a map and chat with them directly around the globe.

Features:
* Tracking of your location
* View users around you via a list or a built-in map
* Search anywhere in the world to find other users
* User profiles with personal data and an avatar
* Become friends with other users and find their current location
* Chat with your friends
